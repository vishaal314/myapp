# Document Scanner Violations Guide

## What Constitutes Violations in Document Scanning

The Document Scanner identifies violations across three major compliance frameworks:

## 1. GDPR Core Principles Violations (Article 5)

### Lawfulness Violations
- **No legal basis specified** for data processing
- **Unlawful processing** without consent, contract, or legal obligation
- **Unauthorized collection** of personal data

### Fairness & Transparency Violations
- **Hidden data collection** without user knowledge
- **Deceptive practices** in data gathering
- **Missing privacy notices** or data protection information

### Purpose Limitation Violations
- **Purpose creep** - using data beyond original purpose
- **Secondary use** without additional consent
- **Incompatible processing** for different purposes

### Data Minimization Violations
- **Excessive data collection** beyond what's necessary
- **Data hoarding** - keeping unnecessary information
- **Over-collection** of personal information

### Accuracy Violations
- **Inaccurate data** not corrected or updated
- **Outdated information** kept without validation
- **Data quality issues** affecting decisions

### Storage Limitation Violations
- **Indefinite storage** without deletion policies
- **Excessive retention** beyond necessary periods
- **No deletion schedules** for personal data

### Security Violations
- **Data breaches** or security incidents
- **Unauthorized access** to personal information
- **Inadequate encryption** or security measures

### Accountability Violations
- **No documentation** of processing activities
- **Compliance failures** without demonstration
- **Inadequate records** of data handling

## 2. GDPR Data Subject Rights Violations

### Missing Rights Implementation
- **Right to access** - no subject access request process
- **Right to rectification** - no data correction mechanism
- **Right to erasure** - no deletion capabilities
- **Right to portability** - no data export function
- **Right to object** - no opt-out mechanisms
- **Automated decision-making** without human oversight

## 3. Special Categories Violations (Article 9)

### Processing Without Legal Basis
- **Health data** without explicit consent
- **Biometric data** for identification without authorization
- **Genetic information** without specific legal basis
- **Racial/ethnic data** without explicit consent
- **Religious beliefs** without consent
- **Political opinions** without authorization

## 4. Netherlands-Specific UAVG Violations

### BSN (Citizen Service Number) Violations
- **Unauthorized BSN processing** without legal mandate
- **BSN collection** without specific authorization
- **Improper BSN handling** not meeting UAVG Article 46

### Minor Consent Violations
- **Processing children's data** under 16 without parental consent
- **Age verification failures** for Dutch users
- **Missing parental authorization** mechanisms

### Medical Data Violations
- **Healthcare data** without explicit Dutch consent requirements
- **Missing medical data safeguards** under UAVG

## 5. EU AI Act Violations

### Prohibited Practices (Article 5) - CRITICAL
- **Subliminal techniques** manipulating behavior
- **Social scoring** systems for citizens
- **Mass biometric surveillance** in public spaces
- **Emotion manipulation** through AI systems

### High-Risk AI Systems (Annex III) - HIGH
- **Biometric identification** without proper safeguards
- **Employment AI** without compliance framework
- **Educational AI** affecting students without protection
- **Credit scoring** without transparency
- **Healthcare AI** without medical oversight

### Transparency Violations (Article 13)
- **AI systems interacting with humans** without disclosure
- **Automated decisions** without transparency notices
- **Missing AI system identification** in interactions

### Fundamental Rights Violations
- **Privacy invasion** through AI processing
- **Algorithmic discrimination** or bias
- **Due process violations** in automated decisions
- **Freedom of expression** restrictions through AI

## 6. International Transfer Violations

### Inadequate Safeguards
- **Third country transfers** without adequacy decisions
- **Missing Standard Contractual Clauses** (SCCs)
- **No Transfer Impact Assessment** (TIA)
- **Inadequate safeguards** for non-EU transfers

## 7. Breach Notification Violations

### Missing Procedures
- **No 72-hour notification** process to authorities
- **Missing data subject notification** procedures
- **Inadequate incident response** plans
- **No breach documentation** processes

## Examples of Common Document Violations

### Healthcare Documents
- Patient records without consent documentation
- Medical data shared without explicit authorization
- Missing data subject rights information

### Employment Documents
- Employee monitoring without transparency
- Recruitment AI without fairness measures
- Background checks without proper legal basis

### Financial Documents
- Credit scoring without explanation
- Automated loan decisions without human review
- Financial data without adequate security

### Educational Documents
- Student profiling without consent
- Educational AI without transparency
- Minor data without parental consent

### Technical Documents
- API documentation exposing PII
- Database schemas with personal data
- Code comments containing real personal information

## Risk Levels

### Critical (Immediate Action Required)
- Prohibited AI practices
- Special category data without legal basis
- Security breaches with personal data

### High (Urgent Attention Needed)
- High-risk AI systems without safeguards
- Missing essential data subject rights
- International transfers without protection

### Medium (Review and Improve)
- Minor transparency issues
- Incomplete documentation
- Partial compliance with principles

### Low (Monitor and Maintain)
- Technical documentation issues
- Minor process improvements needed
- Best practice enhancements

## Compliance Scoring

- **90-100**: Fully Compliant - Minor improvements only
- **70-89**: Mostly Compliant - Some attention needed
- **50-69**: Partially Compliant - Significant work required
- **0-49**: Non-Compliant - Major overhaul needed

## Next Steps After Violations Found

1. **Review detailed findings** in scan results
2. **Prioritize critical and high-risk** violations
3. **Implement recommended safeguards** for each finding
4. **Document compliance measures** taken
5. **Re-scan to verify** improvements
6. **Establish ongoing monitoring** processes

The Document Scanner provides specific recommendations for each violation type to help achieve full compliance across all frameworks.
