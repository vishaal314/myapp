      FIGURE 6: MATHEMATICAL FORMULAS (CORRECTED)
      
 85   BIAS DETECTION ALGORITHMS:
      
      Formula 1 - Demographic Parity:
         P(Y=1|A=0) ≈ P(Y=1|A=1)
         
 90   Formula 2 - Equalized Odds:
         TPR_A=0 ≈ TPR_A=1 AND FPR_A=0 ≈ FPR_A=1
         
      Formula 3 - Calibration Score:
         P(Y=1|Score=s,A=0) ≈ P(Y=1|Score=s,A=1)
         
 95   Formula 4 - Individual Fairness:
         d(f(x1),f(x2)) ≤ L*d(x1,x2)
      
      
      BSN CHECKSUM VALIDATION (CORRECTED - Official Dutch Algorithm):
100   
      checksum = (digit_0 × 9) + (digit_1 × 8) + (digit_2 × 7) + 
                 (digit_3 × 6) + (digit_4 × 5) + (digit_5 × 4) + 
                 (digit_6 × 3) + (digit_7 × 2) - (digit_8 × 1)

265   
      BSN FORMULA CORRECTION:
      
      OLD (INCORRECT):
         checksum = Σ(digit_i × (9-i)) mod 11  ✗ WRONG
270   
      NEW (CORRECT):
         checksum = (digit_0 × 9) + (digit_1 × 8) + ... - (digit_8 × 1) ✓ CORRECT
         
      The last digit (digit_8) uses multiplication factor 1 with subtraction,
275   not derived from the general formula pattern. This is the official Dutch
      government BSN 11-proof algorithm specification.
