## SAMENVATTING (249 WOORDEN)

Een automated vendor risk management systeem voor GDPR Article 28 en Schrems II compliance. De uitvinding valideert 7 contractual requirements: processing instructions (Article 28(3)(a), 20% weight), confidentiality (Article 28(3)(b), 15%), security measures (Article 28(3)(c)+32, 25%), sub-processors (Article 28(3)(d), 15%), data subject rights (Article 28(3)(e), 10%), deletion/return (Article 28(3)(g), 10%), audit rights (Article 28(3)(h), 5%). Compliance percentage: (validated_weight / total_weight) × 100, artikel_28_compliant when ≥95%.

Schrems II transfer impact assessment classificeert processing locations: EU/EEA (safe), adequate_country (safe), USA_Privacy_Shield (INVALID - Schrems II CJEU C-311/18), USA_DPF (valid), non_adequate_country (requires SCCs/BCRs), unknown (high_risk). Transfer mechanism validation detecteert prohibited transfers en missing safeguards (SCCs, BCRs, adequacy decisions).

Comprehensive risk scoring algorithm: overall_risk_score = (security × 0.30) + (compliance × 0.25) + (data_processing × 0.25) + (financial_stability × 0.10) + (service_quality × 0.10). Risk level classification: MINIMAL ≥80, LOW ≥60, MEDIUM ≥40, HIGH ≥20, CRITICAL <20. Approval logic: approved_for_use = (overall ≥60) AND (compliance ≥70) AND (security ≥65).

**Netherlands AP integration**: Article 27 local representative validation (non-EU vendors processing Netherlands data), AP notification templates, data residency verification (EU/EEA preference), regional penalty multipliers.

Automated vendor categorization: 7 types (data_processor Article 28, joint_controller Article 26, cloud_provider Article 28+32, saas_provider, marketing_partner Article 28+6, sub_processor, consulting_service) met type-specific criteria en minimum compliance thresholds (data_processor: 90%, cloud_provider: 85%, marketing_partner: 80%).

Security score calculation: encryption (25 points), access controls (20), audit logging (15), incident response (15), business continuity (10), security testing (15), certifications bonus (ISO27001 +10, SOC2 +10).

Performance: 75% time savings (2-3 hours vs 8-12 hours), 85% cost reduction (€500-€1,500 vs €3,000-€10,000), annual savings €50K-€200K for 20-50 vendors.
