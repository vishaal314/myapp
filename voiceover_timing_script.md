## **Audio Recording Technical Specifications**

### **Equipment Setup**
- **Microphone**: USB condenser or XLR with audio interface
- **Position**: 6-8 inches from mouth, slightly off-axis
- **Environment**: Quiet room, minimal echo/reverb
- **Monitoring**: Closed-back headphones for monitoring

### **Recording Settings**
- **Sample Rate**: 48 kHz
- **Bit Depth**: 24-bit
- **Format**: WAV (uncompressed)
- **Levels**: Peak at -6dB to -12dB (avoid clipping)

### **Quality Control Checklist**
- [ ] No background noise or hum
- [ ] Consistent volume levels throughout
- [ ] Clear articulation of all words
- [ ] Proper emphasis on key terms
- [ ] Natural pacing and flow
- [ ] No mouth sounds or breathing noise
- [ ] Room tone recorded for editing

### **Post-Recording Audio Processing**
1. **Noise Reduction**: Remove any background noise
2. **EQ**: Gentle high-pass filter at 80-100Hz
3. **Compression**: Light compression for consistency
4. **Normalization**: Normalize to -16 LUFS for video
5. **De-essing**: Remove harsh sibilants if needed
