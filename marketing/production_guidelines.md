### Technical Requirements

#### Video Formats by Platform
```
LinkedIn/YouTube:     1920x1080 (16:9) - Landscape

Instagram Stories:    1080x1920 (9:16) - Portrait
```

#### Video Settings
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps minimum, 60fps preferred
- **Bitrate:** 8-15 Mbps for high quality
- **Codec:** H.264 for maximum compatibility
- **Audio:** 48kHz, 16-bit minimum, stereo
- **File Size:** <100MB for most platforms, <500MB for YouTube

#### Duration Guidelines
- **LinkedIn:** 30-120 seconds (90 seconds optimal)
- **Twitter/X:** 15-60 seconds (30 seconds optimal)
- **Instagram Feed:** 15-90 seconds (45 seconds optimal)
- **Instagram Stories:** 15 seconds maximum
- **TikTok:** 15-60 seconds (30 seconds optimal)
- **YouTube:** 60-180 seconds (120 seconds optimal)

### Voiceover Specifications
- **Language:** English primary, Dutch secondary
- **Tone:** Professional but approachable
- **Pace:** 150-180 words per minute
- **Volume:** -12dB to -6dB peak levels
- **Quality:** Broadcast quality, noise-free

### Background Music
- **Style:** Corporate, upbeat, non-intrusive
- **Volume:** -24dB to -18dB (under voiceover)
- **License:** Royalty-free required
- **Sources:** Epidemic Sound, AudioJungle, Freesound

### Sound Effects
- **Interface Clicks:** Subtle UI interaction sounds
- **Notification Sounds:** Alert tones for violations
- **Success Sounds:** Positive chimes for compliance
- **Volume:** -30dB to -24dB (minimal)
